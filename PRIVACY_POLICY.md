# Privacy Policy for DetoxYT Extension

DetoxYT respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection
- The extension does NOT collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, passwords, or browsing history.

## Local Storage
- The extension stores user preferences (such as selected topic and extension status) locally using the browser's storage API.
- This data is stored only on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

## Third-Party Services
- The extension may fetch publicly available YouTube video data to display relevant content.
- No personal user data is shared with any third-party services.

## Permissions Usage
- The extension interacts with YouTube pages to filter and modify content for productivity purposes.

## Data Security
- No sensitive user data is collected or stored.

## Changes
This privacy policy may be updated in the future.
